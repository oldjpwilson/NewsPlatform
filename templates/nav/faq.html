How to earn money with NewsPlatform

  • NewsPlatform allows users create channels for free.
  • The users then publish articles on their channels.
  • If the articles are of a high quality, others users will subscribe to a channel to read articles.
  • After you have 20 subscribers, you will earn USD $0.25 per subscriber per month.
  • You may create as many channels, and publish as many articles as you want, but people will only pay for high quality, valuable articles.
